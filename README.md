**SMART EB STREET LIGHT**

Smart EB Street Light is an intelligent street lighting system designed to improve energy efficiency, public safety, and environmental sustainability. The system automatically switches OFF during daytime and turns ON at nighttime using a light sensor, eliminating unnecessary power consumption.

To further save energy, the streetlight operates at a dim brightness level during the night when no activity is detected. When a human, animal, or vehicle passes by, motion sensors detect the movement and instantly increase the brightness of the light, providing better visibility and safety. After a specified period of inactivity, the light returns to dim mode, reducing energy usage.

The system is equipped with a CCTV camera that provides 24/7 monitoring of streets and public areas. This feature helps improve security and assists in surveillance when required. An emergency buzzer is also integrated into the system. In emergency situations, a person can press the buzzer, which triggers an alert message to the nearest police station or concerned authorities, enabling a faster response.

To make the system environmentally friendly, solar panels are used to generate electricity during the day. The generated energy is stored in rechargeable batteries and utilized to power the streetlights and other components at night. This reduces dependence on conventional electricity and lowers operational costs.

The Smart EB Street Light system combines automation, security, and renewable energy technologies into a single solution. It is highly suitable for smart cities, highways, residential areas, campuses, and public spaces. By reducing power consumption, enhancing safety, and promoting the use of renewable energy, the system offers a reliable, cost-effective, and sustainable approach to modern street lighting.
